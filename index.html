How to play - Rules of the game

Each turn, a player can draw a Pokémon card three times for himself and an opponent. Once drawn three times, the 'generate' button is locked. If the player has selected a card for himself as well as his opponent, he can press the "Fight" button. The card whose sum of stats is greater than the opposite card wins the duel.

After winning the duel, there is another turn where the player can again draw cards three times for himself and his opponent. To proceed to the next duel, a new card must be drawn for your opponent. Losing a duel with an opponent's card results in the end of the game. To play again, after losing a duel, press the 'New Game' button in the window that appears.

To turn the in-game music on or off, press the buttons in the top left corner. By default, music are turned off. To start the game, choose your hero by clicking on the pokeball under the character's name.
Enjoy the game!
